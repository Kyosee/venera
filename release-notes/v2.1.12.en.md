## What's Changed

### Added
- AI comic-page translation: while reading, page text can be translated and overlaid directly on the image, keeping the original artwork. Turned on per comic.
- Pre-translate whole chapters ahead of time from a comic's menu, with a chapter picker and live progress; already-translated chapters are recognized across restarts.
- Per-comic glossary: view and edit translated terms to keep names consistent across chapters, with a blocked-terms list for words you don't want translated.
- Test translation button in settings to check your endpoint with a sample line before reading.
- Choice of translation target language, now including Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish and Russian.
- Downloadable on-device text-recognition models, organized by text detection and text recognition.
- A help entry in AI translation settings that explains setup and usage in plain language.
- WebDAV sync gains a "sync local comic library" toggle.
- Progress bar while checking for follow-updates.

### Changed
- Translation results are now saved permanently and included in sync, so a page is only translated once.
- Vertical text is now supported when overlaying translations on tall, narrow CJK bubbles.
- Before pre-translating multiple chapters, an estimate of request volume is shown to avoid unexpected cost on paid endpoints.
- Refined the translation prompt for a more natural, colloquial manga tone.
- Minor icon visual tweak for a more comfortable look.

### Fixed
- Batch update check no longer misses comics with non-standard version numbers and wrongly reports no update.
- Moving with one finger after zooming into an image no longer accidentally triggers swipe-to-favorite.
- The reader top bar now reflects translating / failed status in real time.
